Scan Licence - Named

Fixed Scan Licences


Fix scanning privelidges to chosen accounts. If you want to make sure one person always has access to scan documents in to FileDirector you can use a named scan licence. You can purchase as many of these as you need and it fixes scanning to the licenced account. These can be paired with concurrent licences for flexibility in the workforce and ensuring that your FileDirector document management system is efficient and easy.
